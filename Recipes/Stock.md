- Making a beef stock in the instant pot. I used two bone in beef shanks. Browned on the sauté
setting, then cooked on the "stock" setting (30 min at high pressure), with natural venting. I
also added an onion cut in 8ths, salt, and about a cup of frozen chicken stock. Added water to
cover. meat came out tender but not dry. I'm going to keep it for taco filling or something. I
threw the bones and chewy bits into the stock pot with the chicken bones from our dinner. I
I also did chicken stock in the instant pot (chicken thighs and onion for 30 min at high
