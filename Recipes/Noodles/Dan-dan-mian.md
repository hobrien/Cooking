Dan Dan Mian

Adapted from [Chinese Cooking Demystified](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-BXJcCLCugo)

Toast, then grind:

- 1/2 tsp sichuan pepper

Combine in small pot and heat until disolved:

- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp black vinegar
- 1.5 tbsp sugar
- 0.5 tsp msg

Add to a small bowl:

- 1.5 tbsp sesame paste

Gradually stir in seasoned soy sauce, then add ground sichuan pepper

Stir in:

- 2 tbsp chili oil

Hand mince:

- 2 slices pork belly:

Add to pork to small bowl and mix in:

- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp corn starch
- 1/2 tsp sesame oil
- 1/2 tsp liaojiu
- 1/2 tsp soy sauce

Marinate at least 10 minutes

Heat over low heat:

- 1 tbsp raw rapeseed oil

Turn up heat and fry until fragrant and starting to darken:

- 4 to 5 deseeded dried chilies

Remove chilies and fry pork mixture until starting to turn brown and crispy, then add:

- 3 tbsp yacai

Continue to stirfry for 1 or 2 minutes, then add around the edges of then wok:

- 0.5 tbsp liaojiu
- 0.5 tbsp soy sauce

Cook in boiling water:

- 400 g noodles

Add 2 tbsp sauce to bottom of bowl, then half of the noodles, then 1 to 2 tbsp of meat mixture

Garnish with:

- sliced scallions

Serve with stir fried [bok choi](../Stir-fries/bok-choi.md)
