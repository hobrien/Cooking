# Wood Ear Salad

Adapted from The Food of Sichuan

Soak in boiled water for 30 min or cold water for several hours to overnight:

- 20 g dried wood ears

Drain and tear into bite sized pieces then blanch for 10–20 seconds.
Shock in cold water, then drain.

In a large bowl combine:

- 2 tbsp thinly sliced red chilli
- 3 tbsp rice wine
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1.5 tsp sesame oil

Add blanched wood ears, along with:

- 2 tsp finely minced garlic
- 1 handful chopped cilantro

Toss to combine and serve.
