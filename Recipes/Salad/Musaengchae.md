# Musaengchae (Korean Radish Banchan)

From [itshelenmelon](https://www.facebook.com/reel/1269808311498133).

Peel, halve lengthways, and cut into thin matchsticks:

- 1 Korean radish (mu), or daikon as a substitute

Toss with:

- 1 tbsp salt

Rinse radish and mix with:

- 2–3 tsp gochugaru
- 1 tbsp minced garlic (4–5 cloves)
- 1 tbsp white vinegar
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1–2 tbsp green onions, chopped

Garnish with:

- 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds

Ready to eat immediately.

## Notes

- Look for a long, oval radish with a smooth surface and a slight
  green gradient near the top. In-season in fall and winter.
- Adjust gochugaru to taste for heat level.
- Adjust sugar to taste — 1 tsp makes it lightly sweet.
