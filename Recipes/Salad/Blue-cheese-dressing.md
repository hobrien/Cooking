# Blue Cheese Dressing

Adapted from [Not Another Cooking Show](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7TRISjx8p_Q)

Combine in a medium bowl to make ranch dressing:

- 1/4 cup mayonnaise
- 1/4 cup sour cream
- 1 tbsp mustard
- 1 clove garlic, finely grated
- 1 tbsp finely grated onion
- 1/2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1/2 tsp mixed herbs
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tsp salt

Freeze for 30 min, then grate into dressing:

- 150 g blue cheese

Stir to combine.

## Notes

- Original recipe called for powdered mustard, garlic, and onion, plus
  buttermilk to achieve a slightly runny texture. Since I used fresh
  ingredients there was enough liquid to get the texture I wanted without
  adding any buttermilk.
