# Schiacciata

Adapted from
[Not Another Cooking Show](https://www.notanothercookingshow.tv/post/the-worlds-most-famous-sandwich)
by reducing volume to fit a half sheet tray.

Mix up poolish the night before:

- 175 g bread flour
- 175 g water
- 1/4 tsp yeast

The next morning, mix bread ingredients:

- 350 g poolish
- 175 g water
- 350 g AP flour
- 4 g salt

Knead for 5 minutes, then scrape into a bowl with a generous amount of
extra virgin olive oil.

Bulk ferment for 3 hours, with periodic stretch and folds.

Line a half sheet tray with baking paper, then coat in EVOO. Add dough
and spread out with fingers.

Rest 15 minutes, then spread dough into corners of pan. Cover with
plastic wrap and proof until bubbly.

Preheat oven to max temp. Cover the dough with a generous sprinkle of
flaky salt. Bake 20 minutes until brown, rotating after 10 minutes.
