# Fish Fragrant Eggplant

Adapted from [Serious Eats](https://www.seriouseats.com/fish-fragrant-eggplants-sichuan-braised-eggplant-with-garlic-ginger-and-chilies).

Toss with salt and leave for 30 minutes:

- 600 g eggplant, cut into 2 cm by 5 cm batons

Rinse and drain eggplant, then stir-fry in batches.

Remove eggplant from wok and cook over medium heat until fragrant
and oil is stained red:

- 3 tbsp raw rapeseed oil
- 1.5 tbsp doubanjiang
- 1.5 tbsp minced garlic
- 1 tbsp minced ginger

Add sauce ingredients:

- 150 ml hot stock or water
- 4 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp light soy sauce

Return eggplant to wok, heat through, and thicken with:

- 3/4 tsp potato starch, mixed with 1 tbsp water

Finish with:

- 1 tbsp black vinegar
- 6 tbsp scallion greens
