#Bok Choi

Blanch stems in boiling water with leaves out of water until starting to soften (ca. 5 minutes):

- 5-6 bok choi

Press leaves into water for 15 seconds

Remove from water and slice bok choi in half length-wise

Heat wok to smoking, add a thin layer of oil, then place bok choi in wok cut side down with stems facing toward the middle, the season with:

- salt

Cook until browned on cut side of stem and serve
