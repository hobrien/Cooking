# Tiger Skin Peppers

Adapted from The Food of Sichuan

Lightly crush and chop into 6–7 cm sections:

- 300 g mild green chilis

Deep fry at 200C until skins are white and blistered.

Drain oil, then return peppers to wok and stir on high heat while
seasoning with:

- Salt

Combine in a small bowl:

- 1 tbsp black vinegar
- 2 tsp soy sauce

Place peppers in a serving dish and pour over sauce.
