# Crispy Beef Strips

Adapted from [Souped Up Recipes](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v2KanVGHFW8)

Blend spice mix ingredients in a blender:

- 10 chilis, seeded
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 star anise
- 1 small stick of cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp fennel seeds
- 1/2 tsp white peppercorn
- 1 tbsp Sichuan peppercorn, seeds removed
- 1/2 of black cardamom

Add to spice mix:

- 2 tbsp sesame seeds
- 1.5 tsp salt
- 1.5 tsp sugar

Add to pot and bring to a boil:

- 1.5 pounds of beef (brisket or round), cut into 4 pieces at slight
  angle to the grain
- 2 tbsp liaojiu
- Few slices of ginger
- 2 pieces of scallion
- Water to cover

Bring to a boil, skim, then simmer on low covered for 15 minutes.

Rinse beef and slice into 1/3 cm slices at slight angle to grain, then
slice into strips.

Fry until crispy in:

- 1 cup raw rapeseed oil

Strain beef and remove oil.

Add to wok with beef and spice mix:

- 1/4 cup scallion oil
- 1/4 cup water

Cook on low until water has evaporated.

Chill beef in fridge for 3 hours.
