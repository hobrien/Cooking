# Mapo Tofu

Adapted from [Chef Wang Gang](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KpnLLhBVIjk).

Slice into 2 cm cubes:

- 600 g pack medium tofu

Cover in cold water then bring to a boil. Cover and turn off heat.

Toast over low heat:

- 5 g dried chilies
- 2.5 g Sichuan pepper, seeds removed

Cut chilies and peppers with knife, or blitz briefly in food
processor/blender.

Mince together and place in small bowl:

- 2 tbsp doubanjiang
- 1 tbsp pickled chilies
- 1 tbsp douchi
- 1 inch ginger
- white portion of 2–3 scallions (optional)

Finely mince:

- 75 g pork belly

Heat wok to smoking, add:

- 2 tbsp cold pressed canola oil

Add pork and cook until crispy.

Add aromatics and cook until oil is stained red, then add around the
sides of the wok:

- 1 tbsp liaojiu
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce

Drain tofu and add to wok. Cover with cold water.

Add knifed chilies.

Add seasoning:

- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp MSG

Thicken with a slurry of:

- 2 tbsp water
- 1 tsp cornstarch

Add:

- green portion of 2–3 scallions, chopped

Serve over rice.
