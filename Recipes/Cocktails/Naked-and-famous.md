# Naked and Famous

Created by Joaquín Simó (Death and Co). Last Word riff (equal parts sour, 
base spirit, herbal liqueur, sweet liqueur).

Shake:

- 3/4 oz lime juice
- 3/4 oz mezcal
- 3/4 oz yellow Chartreuse
- 3/4 oz Aperol

Double strain into a 5.5 oz Nick and Nora; no garnish.

## Notes

- I don't have yellow Chartreuse, so I used green.
