# Whiskey Sour

Created by Joaquín Simó (Death and Co). Last Word riff (equal parts sour, 
base spirit, herbal liqueur, sweet liqueur).

Dry shake, then shake with ice:

- 1 egg white
- 1 oz lemon juice
- 3/4 oz simple syruo (1:1)
- 2 oz whiskey

Double strain into a chilled 7.5 oz coup; garnish with 4 drops of bitters
