# Creamy Black Lentils

Adapted from the Curry Lover's Cookbook.

Soak overnight, then drain:

- 175 g washed urid dal

Simmer in a large pan until tender, then mash and cool:

- soaked dal
- 50 g red split lentils
- water to cover

Stir into the lentils:

- 120 ml double cream
- 120 ml natural yogurt
- 1 tsp cornstarch

Fry in 1 tbsp ghee or oil until onion is soft:

- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 1 piece fresh root ginger (5 cm), crushed
- 2 fresh green chilies, chopped
- 1 tomato, chopped

Add and fry for 2 minutes:

- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
- 1/2 tsp ground cumin
- pinch of salt

Stir onion mixture into lentils and reheat. Transfer to a serving dish.

Fry in remaining 2 tbsp ghee or oil over low heat until golden:

- 2 garlic cloves, sliced
- 2 fresh green chilies, chopped

Pour over lentils and fold in just before serving. Garnish with
coriander sprigs and sliced red chili. Serve with extra cream on
the side.


## Instant pot version

Cook for 10 minutes in instant pot with rapid venting:

- 175 g washed urid dal
- 600 ml water

Add and cook for 10 minutes:

- 50 g red split lentils

Follow remaining steps above, finishing dish in instant pot
