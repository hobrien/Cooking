# Cincinnati Chili

Adapted from Cook's Illustrated Soups and Stews.

Boil in salted water until floating:

- 500 g 20% beef mince

Sauté:

- 2 medium onions, chopped fine (about 2 cups)

Add:

- 2 medium cloves garlic, minced

Combine and add to onions and garlic:

- 2 tbsp chili powder
- 2 tsp mixed herbs
- 2 tsp cocoa powder
- 1.5 tsp cinnamon
- 0.5 tsp cayenne pepper
- 0.5 tsp allspice
- 0.5 tsp black pepper

Add and simmer for 20 minutes:

- 2 cups tomato sauce
- 2 cups canned low-sodium chicken broth
- 2 tbsp cider vinegar
- 2 tsp dark brown sugar

Serve with:

- 1 pound spaghetti, cooked, drained, and tossed with 2 tablespoons
  unsalted butter
- 12 ounces sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
- 1 (15-ounce) can red kidney beans, drained, rinsed, and warmed
- 1 medium onion, chopped fine (about 1 cup)

Notes:

- The mince from Sainsbury's doesn't work for this because it is
  compressed into a solid block.
