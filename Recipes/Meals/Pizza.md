# Pizza

Adapted from [Josh Weissman](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yMfOyJeIz8c&t=162s)
and [Vincenzo's Plate](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8Q_9h6VKm9c)

Combine and knead together:
- 100 g starter
- 250 g water 
- 450 g 00 flour 
- 15 g salt

Bulk ferment for 2-6 hours

Divide into 3 dough balls and proof until the next day at 16-18C
