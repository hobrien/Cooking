# Bolognese

Adapted from [Not Another Cooking Show](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=caIQBp-6OL4).

Process separately in food processor:

- 2 carrots, chopped
- 3 stalks celery, chopped
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 5 cloves garlic (can be processed with onion)

Cook out moisture from vegetables (approximately 20 minutes) along
with:

- olive oil to coat pan
- salt and pepper to taste

Add and cook out moisture:

- 1 kg 20% fat beef mince

Add and cook until dark fond develops:

- 1 cup tomato paste

Deglaze pan, then reduce by half:

- 1/2 bottle red wine

Add and cook 3–4 hours, stirring every hour and adding more water
if necessary:

- water to cover
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 bunch thyme
- parmesan rind (if you have it)

Remove herbs and stir in:

- 1/4 cup cream
- 1/2 cup grated parmesan
