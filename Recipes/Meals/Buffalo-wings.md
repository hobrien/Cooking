# Buffalo Wings

Adapted from [Chinese Cooking Demystified](https://chinesecookingdemystified.substack.com/p/why-china-hates-buffalo-wings-and)

Combine marinade ingredients in a large bowl:

- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp chicken bouillon powder
- 1/2 tbsp Kashmiri chili powder or Gochugaru
- 1/2 tbsp cayenne
- 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1/2 tbsp bourbon
- 1/2 tbsp tabasco
- 1/2 tsp white pepper

Add to bowl and coat in marinade:

- 600 g chicken wings

Marinate for at least 90 minutes.

Blend together:

- 80 g pickled chilis
- 3 garlic cloves, crushed
- 1/3 cup water

Press through fine mesh strainer and add:

- 1/2 tbsp milk powder
- 1/2 tbsp Tabasco
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp rice vinegar
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1/4 tsp MSG

Mix together, then add to marinated chicken wings:

- 1 egg white, thoroughly beaten
- 10 g corn starch
- 10 g water

Deep fry chicken wings at 120C for 1 minute.

Remove and heat oil to 175C. Add wings back to the oil and fry until brown and crispy, about 5 minutes.

Melt in wok:

- 2 tbsp butter

Add and fry until fragrant:

- 1 clove garlic, finely minced
- 2 sliced chilis

Add:

- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

Add around sides of wok:

- 1 tbsp bourbon

Add sauce to wok, bring to a simmer, then add slurry of:

- 2 tsp potato starch
- 2 tsp water

Once thickened, take off heat and mix in:

- 2.5 tbsp cold butter, cut into cubes

Serve wings with sauce, [blue cheese dressing](../Salad/Blue-cheese-dressing.md), and carrot/celery sticks.
