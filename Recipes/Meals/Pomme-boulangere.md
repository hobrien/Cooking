# Pomme Boulangère

Adapted from [Fallow](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-Z3P_NujFos)

Thin slice and caramalise:

- 4 medium onions

Mix in:

- clarified butter
- beef stock

Layer:

- 6 thin sliced potatoes
- clarified butter
- beef stock
- salt

Add to one of the middle layers

- minced rosemary
- pepper

Cover with cartouche and a small lid and bake for 1 hour at 120C

Remove cover and cook for an additional 30 min

Notes

- This turned out OK, but not as nice as Pommes Anna
