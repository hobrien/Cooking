# Chocolate Layer Cake

Adapted from [Serious Eats](https://www.seriouseats.com/easy-chocolate-layer-cake-with-chocolate-frosting-recipe)

Preheat oven to 350 F

Grease 4 17 cm cake pans and line bottoms with grease proof paper. Prepare cake strips and wrap around outside of pans

Sift together into a large bowl:

- 195 g all purpose flour
- 42 g cocoa powder
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/8 tsp salt

Cream together:

- 250 g granulated sugar
- 287 g sour cream
- 150 g vegetable oil
- 2 large eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp coffee extract

Mix wet ingredients into dry ingredients. Sive into cake pans (250 g each)

Bake on two oven racks for appprox 15 minutes, rotating after 7 minutes, until firm and inserted toothpick comes out clean. Let cool 5 minutes before inverting onto cooling racks.
