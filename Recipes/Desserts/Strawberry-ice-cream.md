# Strawberry Ice Cream

Blend:

- 110 g fresh strawberries
- 30 g sugar
- 1/2 tsp vanilla
- pinch salt

Strain through a fine mesh strainer to remove seeds, then stir in:

- 100 g double cream

Chill 2 hours, then pour into ice cream maker

# Notes

- I worked from the recipe included with the ice cream maker, but reduced the sugar and added WAY more strawberries, removing the milk to compensate
