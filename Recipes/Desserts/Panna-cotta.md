# Panna Cotta

Recipe adapted from
[Serious Eats](https://www.seriouseats.com/classic-panna-cotta-vanilla-bean-recipe).

Rub together and leave overnight:

- 50 g sugar
- scant 1/4 tsp salt
- seeds from 1 vanilla pod

Heat to steaming and steep overnight in fridge:

- 300 ml whipping cream
- 180 ml milk
- vanilla pod (without seeds)

Bloom gelatin for 10 minutes:

- 5 g gelatin
- 1/4 cup milk
- scant 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Heat milk and cream to steaming, then mix in sugar and gelatin. Strain
into a large measuring cup and allow to cool until lukewarm.

Pour into 6 ramekins or small bowls. Allow to set in fridge for at
least 6 hours.

Serve with fruit compote.
