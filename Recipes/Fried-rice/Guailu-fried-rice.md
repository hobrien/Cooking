# Guailu Fried Rice

Adapted from
[Chinese Cooking Demystified](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_hkG0e13HKM).

Pound in a mortar and pestle:

- 1 tbsp pickled chilies
- white portion of 2–3 scallions, sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, smashed
- 1 inch ginger, diced

Fry in oil or lard:

- 50 g pancetta

Add aromatics and fry 3–5 minutes until fat starts to separate and
turn clear.

Add and fry for 2 minutes:

- 15 g suancai/sauerkraut
- small can of rinsed kidney beans

Add and fry until hot and separated:

- one batch day-old rice or [steamed rice](Steamed-rice)

Add around edges of wok:

- 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 1 tsp light soy sauce

Season with:

- 1/8 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1/8 tsp MSG

Mix in:

- green portion of 2–3 scallions
- 25 g cilantro leaves/stems/roots

Add around edges of wok:

- 2 tsp dark vinegar
