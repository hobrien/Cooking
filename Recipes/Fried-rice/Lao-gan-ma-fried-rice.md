# Lao Gan Ma Fried Rice

Adapted from
[Chinese Cooking Demystified](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2KHAuPqDYyg).

Stirfry until charred:

- 40 g sliced napa cabbage

Add around the edges of th wok:

- 1/2 tbsp liaojiu

Remove cabbage

Fry util fat is rendered:

- 3 slices bacon, chopped into strips
- 3 tbsp water
- 1/2 tbsp oil

Add to wok and fry until fragrent:

- white portion of 2–3 scallions, sliced
- 1 cloves garlic, smashed
- 1/2 inch ginger, diced

Add around the edges of the wok, then reduce:

- 1/2 tbsp liaojiu

Add and fry until fragrant:

- 2 tsp Kashmirir chili/Gochugaru

Add and mix:

- 2 tbsp Lao Gan Ma with black beans

Add and stirfry over high heat until hot and separated:

- one batch day-old rice or [steamed rice](Steamed-rice)

Add and fry for 2 minutes:

- 1 tbsp slight soy sauce
- small can of rinsed kidney beans
- cabbage

Season with:

- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp MSG
- 1/4 tsp white pepper

Mix in:

- green portion of 2–3 scallions
