# Steamed Rice

- This rice can be used immediately for fried rice.
- From [Chinese Cooking Demystified](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=owUiKyx4chI)

Wash:

- 230 g jasmine rice

Par-boil in a pot of boiling water for 3 minutes until grains are
opaque.

Drain rice in a colander. Spread evenly and poke 6–8 holes in rice
with a chopstick, then place over 2 inches of boiling water. Cover
pot with a towel and lid, then steam for 10 minutes plus 5 minutes
off heat.

Fluff rice with a fork and spread onto a large plate.

## Notes

- Can also cover colander with foil rather than using a towel.
